About the Game

Welcome to Longleaf Valley: Merge Puzzle, where your strategic thinking meets your love for nature! Help restore a once-thriving valley by merging objects, solving puzzles, and reviving flora and fauna. Each level brings a new challenge and an opportunity to bring harmony back to this enchanting ecosystem.
