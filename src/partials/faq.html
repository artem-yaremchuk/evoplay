FAQ

  • Is Longleaf Valley free to play?

    Yes, the game is free to download and play, with optional in-app purchases for faster progress.

  • Can I play offline?

    Absolutely! You can enjoy restoring the valley even without an internet connection.

  • Are there updates with new content?

    Yes, seasonal events and regular updates bring new challenges, areas, and rewards.

  • What makes this game unique?

    It’s not just a puzzle game; it’s a mission to restore and protect a beautiful virtual ecosystem while having fun.
